📝 语法要点：Past Simple 过去式

  • 规则变化（加-ed）：fish → fished, learn → learnt, skip → skipped, kick → kicked
  • 不规则变化（要背！）：fly → flew, drive → drove, throw → threw, catch → caught

🐾 动物比较 — Comparative Adverbs
🔊

📖 例句讲解

🔊
Elephants can move faster than snakes.
大象比蛇移动得更快。 🔊
faster /ˈfæstər/ = 更快
🔊
Monkeys can climb better than tigers.
猴子比老虎爬得更好。 🔊
better /ˈbetər/ = 更好
🔊
Bears can run more quickly than monkeys.
熊比猴子跑得更快。 🔊
quickly /ˈkwɪkli/ = 快速地（长副词用more）

📝 规则总结：副词比较级

  • 短副词（1个词）→ 加-er：fast → faster, hard → harder
  • 长副词（2个词以上）→ 前面加more：quickly → more quickly, carefully → more carefully
  • 特殊的：well → better, badly → worse

🔊

🔊
– from the bottom of the sea to the highest mountain.
——从海底到最高的山顶。 🔊

Most plants make food using sunlight.
大多数植物用阳光制造食物。 🔊

They also need water to survive, just like us!
它们也需要水才能活下来，就像我们一样！ 🔊

Plants usually have three parts to them: roots, a stem and leaves.
植物通常有三个部分：根、茎和叶子。 🔊

有些植物还有花或果实。 🔊

🪴 Culture: Carnivorous Plants 食虫植物
🔊

🌍 什么是食虫植物？

🔊
Like most other plants, carnivorous plants need sun, air and water to make their own food.
和大多数植物一样，食虫植物需要阳光、空气和水来制造食物。 🔊
carnivorous /kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/ = 食虫的
🔊
But they also get extra nutrients by eating small animals!
但它们还通过吃小动物来获取额外的营养！ 🔊
nutrients /ˈnuːtriənts/ = 营养

🪤 Venus Flytrap 捕蝇草

🔊
Venus flytraps grow naturally in the USA but are used as house plants all over the world.
捕蝇草自然生长在美国，但世界各地都有人当盆栽养。 🔊
🔊
They use their leaves to catch insects, like flies.
它们用叶子抓虫子，比如苍蝇。 🔊
insects /ˈɪnsekts/ = 昆虫
🔊
The leaves close when an insect touches the tiny hairs that grow on them.
当虫子碰到叶子上的小毛毛时，叶子就会合上。 🔊
🔊
The plant can then slowly eat the insect.
然后植物就会慢慢把虫子吃掉。 🔊

🌿 Sundew Plant 茅膏菜

🔊
Sundew plants are found in the Indian rainforests.
茅膏菜生长在印度的热带雨林里。 🔊
rainforests /ˈreɪnfɒrɪsts/ = 热带雨林
🔊
Their leaves have sticky hairs
它们的叶子上有粘粘的毛 🔊
sticky /ˈstɪki/ = 粘粘的
🔊
and when an insect lands on the leaves, the sticky hairs trap them.
当虫子落在叶子上，粘毛就把它们困住了。 🔊
trap /træp/ = 困住
🔊
Large sundew plants can trap and eat spiders.
大的茅膏菜甚至能困住蜘蛛吃掉！ 🔊
