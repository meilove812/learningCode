💬 讨论题

Q: Do you think the dragon is greedy or hungry? Why?
📖 参考答案
The dragon is hungry, not greedy. He only comes when he has a stomach-ache and is hungry. When there is porridge, he takes it and leaves. He doesn't take more than he needs.
Q: Is Too-too-moo a good friend? Why?
📖 参考答案
Yes, Too-too-moo is a good friend. Even when the dragon was angry and pushed her, she still understood that the dragon was hungry, not greedy. She leaves porridge in the forest for the dragon, showing she still cares.
