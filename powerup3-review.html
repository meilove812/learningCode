📝 Grammar Review 语法复习

1️⃣ 最高级 Superlatives（the + ...est / the most ...）

the highest 最高的
Mount Teide is the highest mountain in Spain.
the biggest 最大的
This is the biggest shop in town.
the longest 最长的
The Nile is the longest river in the world.
the strongest 最强壮的
An elephant is the strongest animal on land.
the best 最好的 ⚠️不规则
She is the best dancer in school.

2️⃣ 比较级 Comparatives（more + ...）

more exciting 更令人兴奋的
English books are more exciting than maths books.

3️⃣ 同级比较 as...as（和...一样）

as clever as 和...一样聪明
I am as clever as my friend Javier.
as high as 和...一样高
I can jump as high as my friend.

4️⃣ could / couldn't（过去的能力）

could ✅ 过去能做
I could swim when I was three.
couldn't ❌ 过去不能做
I couldn't ride a bike when I was three.
